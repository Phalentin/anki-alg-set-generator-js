Syntax Guide:

Notation:

Currently, only 3x3 notation is supported. Besides the standard notation, the following special moves and combinations of them are also possible (obviously not only with R moves):

  • r
  • Rw
  • R*
  • R3 (any number is possible)

Groups and Subgroups

Groups and Subgroups can be created using a hastag like in the following example: 

# Group 1
R U R' U R U2 R'
## A Subgroup
R U R' F' R U R' U' R' F R2 U' R'

The more hashtags there are, the lower order is the subgroup that's created.

Keep in mind, that in the generation process, the group names are converted to all lowercase and spaces are replaced with hyphens.

Algorithm Names

By default, the algorithms are just named with a number corresponding to their order. You can give your algorithms custom names, by typing the name in front of the algorithm and then adding a colon. That would look like this:

Ua-Perm: R2 U' S' U2 S U' R2

Notes

You can add notes to your algorithms by typing an asterisk after the algorithm and then adding your note afterwards. That would look like this:

R2 U' S' U2 S U' R2 *Recognition: full block on left, adjacent colors front

A note will only be shown in Anki, if the answer is revealed.

Combinations

Of course you can also combine algorithm names and notes.
